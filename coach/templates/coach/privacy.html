Privacy Policy

We built Uccharon around a simple idea: your keys, your data, your control. This page explains — in plain language — what we store, what we don't, and the choices you have.

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

Uccharon is an AI-powered English learning and speaking practice platform. It helps you build real fluency by giving structured feedback on everything you write or say. With Uccharon you can:

  • Practice English conversations at your own pace.
  • Receive clear grammar corrections with explanations.
  • Improve your vocabulary and sentence structure.
  • Get pronunciation guidance.
  • Track your learning progress over time.

What makes Uccharon different is that it runs on your own AI. You connect your preferred AI provider using your own API keys and choose the models you want to learn with.

Most important

How We Handle Your API Keys

Your API keys are yours, and they stay with you. Specifically:

  • Uccharon does not store your API keys on its servers.
  • API keys are stored only on your own device, in your browser's local storage.
  • AI requests are sent directly from your browser/device to the AI provider you selected.
  • Uccharon's backend never receives, stores, or accesses your API keys.

Your API keys are never included in any of the following:

  • Conversation history
  • Learning analytics
  • Statistics
  • User data stored on Uccharon servers

Because keys live on your device, you are responsible for managing your own AI provider API keys — keeping them safe, rotating them, and removing them when you no longer need them. No system can promise perfect security, so we won't claim your keys are "100% secure" or "impossible to access." We simply keep them off our servers so there is far less to expose.

AI Provider Usage

Uccharon supports multiple AI providers and models, including:

  • Google Gemini
  • Groq
  • OpenRouter
  • OpenAI
  • Other providers that may be added in the future

When you use your own API key, your requests are processed according to that provider's own privacy policy. Uccharon does not control how third-party AI providers store or process your data. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any AI provider you choose to use.

Conversation Data

To power your learning features, Uccharon stores the data your practice sessions produce. This may include:

  • Your messages
  • AI conversational responses
  • Grammar corrections
  • Vocabulary suggestions
  • Sentence improvements
  • Pronunciation guidance
  • Learning feedback
  • Performance ratings

We store this so we can give you:

  • Conversation history you can revisit
  • A Learning History page
  • Progress tracking
  • Performance analytics
  • A personalized learning experience

Account Information

To make your account work, Uccharon stores basic information such as:

  • Username and email
  • Account information
  • Learning preferences
  • Daily goals
  • Application settings

Analytics and Tracking

Uccharon does not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics services that collect your personal learning data. Your practice stays focused on you — not on ad networks.

Your control

Account Deletion and User Control

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings. When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information
  • Conversations
  • Learning History
  • Grammar corrections
  • Vocabulary suggestions
  • Sentence improvements
  • Pronunciation guidance
  • Performance statistics
  • Progress data
  • User preferences and settings

Your locally stored API keys are also removed from your device during account deletion. After deletion, your account and its associated data cannot be recovered.

Data Security

We take practical steps to protect your data:

  • HTTPS communication is used to protect data in transit.
  • API keys are not stored on Uccharon servers.
  • Authentication protections are in place for accounts.
  • User data is isolated between accounts.

No online service can guarantee absolute security, so we don't make that claim. Instead, we reduce risk by storing as little sensitive data as possible and keeping your keys off our servers entirely.

Third-Party Services

Uccharon relies on external AI providers to generate feedback, such as:

  • Google Gemini
  • Groq
  • OpenRouter
  • OpenAI

When you send requests to these providers, your data is processed under their control and their privacy policies. Please review each provider's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

Open Source Transparency

Uccharon is an open-source project. The source code is publicly available so anyone can review how the application works, including its privacy-related implementations.

github.com/ehtesam75/Uccharon

Your Rights

You stay in control of your experience. At any time you can:

  • Update your account settings.
  • Change AI providers.
  • Manage your learning preferences.
  • Delete your account permanently.
  • Remove locally stored API keys.

Contact

For questions about privacy or data handling, please contact the Uccharon team. You can also open an issue on our public GitHub repository.
