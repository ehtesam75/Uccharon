Open source & privacy-first

The story behind Uccharon

Uccharon is an AI-powered English learning and speaking practice platform built on a simple belief: fluency should be affordable, transparent, and fully in your control. You bring your own AI, your own API key, and your own pace — the tool just gets out of your way.

AI speaking practice Bring your own AI Open & transparent

The motivation

Why Uccharon exists

Great English practice shouldn't mean expensive subscriptions or handing over control of your data. Uccharon started as an answer to a few frustrations.

Learning is often expensive

Many English platforms lock the best practice behind pricey monthly subscriptions that add up fast — especially for students.

Little control over the AI

Most apps decide which model you use and how much you pay, with no way to switch providers or use the free tiers you already have.

Opaque key & privacy handling

It's rarely clear where your API keys go or how your data is handled. Trust shouldn't require guesswork.

A better way to learn

Uccharon was built to offer an affordable, transparent, and user-controlled learning experience — with nothing hidden.

Our mission

Make AI-powered English learning accessible, transparent, and affordable for everyone.

Every decision in Uccharon is measured against that mission — from keeping API keys on your device, to supporting free provider tiers, to publishing the entire codebase in the open.

Where we're headed

Our vision

A learning companion that keeps getting better, shaped by the people who use it.

  • Deeper, more realistic speaking practice
  • Support for more AI providers and models
  • More personalized learning insights
  • Community-driven improvements
  • Continuous enhancement of the learning experience

What guides us

Our philosophy

Four principles that shape how Uccharon is built and how it treats you.

User Control First

You choose your own AI providers and models. No forced defaults, no lock-in — your setup, your call.

Privacy by Design

API keys stay on your device and never touch our servers. We store as little as possible, on purpose.

Open & Transparent

The project is open source and publicly auditable. Anyone can read the code and verify how it works.

Learning Focused

Designed specifically for English improvement and long-term progress — not endless chatter or vanity metrics.

Open source

Nothing hidden

Uccharon is open source. Anyone can inspect exactly how the project works and verify our privacy claims for themselves — because trust should be earned in code, not marketing.
