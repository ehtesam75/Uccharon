The complete Uccharon guide

Everything you need to set up Uccharon, connect your own AI provider, and get the best English learning experience. Follow along from the top, or jump straight to the section you need.

Step one

Getting started

Create your account and get comfortable with the basics. It only takes a couple of minutes.

1

Create an account

Sign up with a username, email, and password. During signup you'll also pick your first AI provider — you can always change it later in Settings.

2

Complete initial setup

Add your AI provider API key so Uccharon can start giving feedback. Your key stays on your device — it's never sent to our servers.

3

Choose your learning goals

Set a daily word goal that fits your schedule. Uccharon tracks it to keep you consistent and build a practice streak.

4

Understand the dashboard

The sidebar holds your conversations, learning history, and statistics. The main area is where you chat. Settings live behind the gear icon.

Bring your own AI

Connect your AI provider

Uccharon runs on your own API key. You choose the provider, you control the cost, and your key never leaves your device.

Google Gemini Generous free tier
Groq Very fast responses
OpenRouter Many models, one key
OpenAI Advanced quality

Add an API key

Open Settings, pick your provider, and paste the key you generated on the provider's dashboard. Each provider tile links straight to where you get your key.

Validate the key

Hit validate and Uccharon runs a quick test call. A green check means you're ready; an error tells you if the key is wrong or the provider is unavailable.

Choose an AI model

Once the key is valid, pick a model from the list. Different models balance speed, depth, and cost — see choosing models below.

Change providers later

Switch providers or models any time in Settings. Add multiple keys and jump between them depending on what you're practicing.

Everyday practice

AI chat practice

The heart of Uccharon — natural conversations that quietly teach you as you go.

  • Have real conversations

    Chat about anything — your day, your work, a topic you love. The more naturally you write, the more useful the feedback.

  • Ask questions freely

    Not sure why something is wrong? Ask. You can request grammar explanations, better phrasing, or examples right in the chat.

  • Receive instant AI feedback

    Every message you send comes back with structured corrections and suggestions alongside the reply, so you learn in context.

  • Improve with regular practice

    Short, frequent sessions beat rare long ones. Keep the conversation going and watch your scores climb over time.

Reading your results

Understanding AI feedback

Feedback is broken into clear sections so you always know exactly what to work on.

Grammar corrections

Fixes tense, agreement, articles, and structure — with a short explanation of the rule behind each change.

Vocabulary improvements

Suggests stronger or more precise word choices, often with synonyms so you can expand your active vocabulary.

Naturalness suggestions

Shows how a native speaker would phrase something, so your English sounds fluent rather than merely correct.

Sentence improvements

Rewrites whole sentences for clarity and flow, giving you a polished version to learn from and reuse.

Pronunciation suggestions

Highlights tricky words with phonetic hints and tips, especially helpful during voice practice.

Mechanics corrections

Catches spelling, punctuation, and capitalization slips — the small details that make writing look sharp.

Transparent scoring

How your score is calculated

Every message you send is rated across five categories. Here's exactly what each one measures and how they combine into your overall score.

Grammar

30%

Looks only at structural correctness: tense, articles, prepositions, subject–verb agreement, word forms, pronouns, conjunctions, modifiers, parallelism, and quantifiers. It does not judge your word choice or how natural you sound.

Vocabulary

20%

Focuses on your word choice — how accurate, appropriate, sophisticated, and varied it is. It does not evaluate collocations or overall phrasing (that's naturalness).

Naturalness

15%

Measures how natural your message sounds in everyday English — collocations, idioms, and common sentence patterns — while avoiding awkward or overly literal translations.

Expression

30%

Rates how effectively you communicate your ideas — completeness, clarity, and level of detail. Simple but clear communication is not penalized; you don't need long or complex sentences to score well.

Mechanics

5%

Checks spelling, capitalization, punctuation, and readability. Casual chat style is fine and won't cost you points unless it actually gets in the way of understanding.

Overall score

100%

Each category is scored from 0 to 100. Your overall score is a weighted average — some categories count more than others, so the final number reflects what matters most for clear, correct English.

The overall score formula

Each category score (0–100) is multiplied by its weight, then added together.

Overall = Grammar × 0.30 + Expression × 0.30 + Vocabulary × 0.20 + Naturalness × 0.15 + Mechanics × 0.05

Speak with confidence

Voice practice

Speak instead of type. Voice practice builds real speaking confidence and sharpens pronunciation.

How it works

  • Enable microphone access. Tap the mic and allow your browser's permission prompt. You can revoke it any time in your browser settings.
  • Pick a speech-to-text option. Choose how your speech is transcribed in Settings, depending on the provider you prefer.
  • Practice speaking naturally. Talk as you would in a real conversation. Regular voice sessions build fluency and reduce hesitation.

Supported voice providers

Browser Speech Recognition OpenAI Whisper Groq Whisper Gemini Audio

Browser Speech Recognition converts your voice to text in real time as you speak and needs no key, while Groq or OpenAI Whisper transcribe your whole speech to text after you finish speaking, using your API key for higher-accuracy results.

Look back to move forward

Learning history

Every correction and suggestion is saved so you can revisit and reinforce what you've learned.

  • Review previous corrections

    Browse all your past grammar, vocabulary, and sentence fixes in one place, grouped by type.

  • Track your improvement

    See how the same mistakes fade over time — proof that your practice is paying off.

  • Revisit vocabulary & suggestions

    Return to suggested words, synonyms, and phrasing so they stick and become part of how you naturally write and speak.

  • Filter by error type

    Narrow your history down to a single category — grammar, vocabulary, naturalness, and more — so you can focus on exactly the kind of mistake you want to work on.

Speed vs. depth

Choosing AI models

Different models suit different moments. Match the model to what you're doing.

Faster models

Great for quick, flowing conversations where you want instant replies and a natural back and forth. Ideal for casual daily practice.

Advanced models

Better for detailed explanations, nuanced corrections, and tricky grammar. Reach for these when you want to really understand the "why".

You can switch models any time in Settings based on your needs — no commitment, no lock-in.

Get more out of it

Tips for better results

A few habits that make a big difference over time.

  • Practice consistently

    A little every day beats occasional marathons. Short sessions keep momentum and your streak alive.

  • Write naturally

    Don't over-edit before you send. Real, unpolished input gives the AI the best chance to help you improve.

  • Review your mistakes

    Revisit your learning history regularly so corrections turn into lasting habits.

  • Use voice mode regularly

    Speaking out loud builds confidence and pronunciation in a way typing can't.

  • Experiment with different models

    Try a fast model for chatting and an advanced one for deep feedback. Find the mix that works for you.

When something goes wrong

Troubleshooting

Common hiccups and quick ways to fix them.

Invalid API key

Double-check for extra spaces or missing characters when pasting. Make sure the key belongs to the provider you've selected, and that it hasn't been revoked. Generate a fresh key from the provider's dashboard if in doubt, then validate it again.

Rate limit reached

You've sent requests faster than your provider's free or paid tier allows. Wait a moment and try again, switch to a different provider or model, or upgrade your plan with the provider for higher limits.

Provider errors

Occasionally a provider has an outage or a model is temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few minutes, pick another model, or switch to a different provider you've set up.

Microphone permission issues

If voice practice can't hear you, check that your browser has microphone permission for Uccharon and that the right input device is selected. Reload the page after granting access, and make sure no other app is holding the microphone.
